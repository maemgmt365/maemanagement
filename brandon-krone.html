Founder

Brandon Krone

Founder & Principal, Mae Management LLC

Full bio

Brandon Krone is the Founder and Principal of Mae Management LLC, a management and acquisitions platform focused on long-term ownership of durable, service-based businesses—primarily within construction, construction management, project management, and federal-adjacent industries.

Brandon’s foundation was built early and hands-on. He grew up immersed in construction, working summers framing homes in Minnesota and gaining firsthand exposure across major trades. That experience instilled a deep respect for craftsmanship, operating discipline, and the people who build and maintain businesses from the ground up.

He competed as a Division I wrestler at the University of Minnesota—an experience that shaped his approach to leadership: consistency, discipline, and doing hard things repeatedly.

After college, Brandon built a career in finance across private wealth and institutional environments, including roles at Fidelity Investments and Charles Schwab. The work sharpened his framework for risk, capital allocation, and long-term decision-making.

Mae Management was founded as a convergence of Brandon’s background in construction, finance, and long-term stewardship. Mae is not a short-term investment vehicle or a roll-up driven by financial engineering. The priority is operational continuity, employee stability, and responsible growth—built on trust and follow-through.

Brandon is based in Austin, Texas and remains actively involved at the strategic level across acquisitions and platform development.

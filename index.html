About Mae Management LLC

Mae Management LLC is a management and acquisitions firm focused on acquiring, operating, and growing established, service-based businesses with durable fundamentals and long-term relevance.

Mae specializes in construction, construction management, project management, environmental services, and federal-adjacent industries—sectors defined by real operations, skilled workforces, and critical infrastructure. These are businesses built over decades, often by founders whose identities are deeply tied to what they’ve created.

Mae Management is not a short-term investment vehicle or a roll-up driven by financial engineering. The firm takes a long-term ownership approach, prioritizing operational continuity, employee stability, and responsible growth. Mae seeks opportunities where disciplined management, thoughtful capital deployment, and strategic advantages—such as federal certifications and Native ownership—can be layered in to enhance scale without compromising culture.

Each acquisition is evaluated not only on financial performance, but on leadership integrity, workforce strength, and alignment with Mae’s stewardship-oriented philosophy. Mae partners with existing teams, installs professional operators where appropriate, and remains actively involved at the strategic level.

Mae engages directly with owners, evaluates opportunities confidentially, and structures transactions to support continuity for teams and customers.

At its core, Mae Management exists to carry businesses forward—not to dismantle them.

Primary targets

We focus on sectors that align with durable demand, contracted revenue, and long-term relevance—especially where federal-adjacent positioning can create sustainable growth.

  • Construction Management & Project Management (CM/PM)
  • Environmental services & remediation / compliance support
  • Facilities services (janitorial, maintenance, light specialty services)
  • …and other federal-adjacent services

Acquisition criteria

  • $500K+ in annual cash flow or earnings
  • Established operations with a track record
  • Recurring customers or contracted revenue
  • Capable management or key operators in place
  • Healthy, explainable margins (market-rate or better)
  • Clear path to responsible growth
  • Clean financials and straightforward operations

Mae Management LLC is a founder-led investment firm headquartered in Austin, Texas, focused on acquiring and operating established construction, project management, and government-adjacent businesses across the United States.

We partner with owners who value continuity, disciplined execution, and a thoughtful transition for their teams and customers. Mae is built for long-term ownership—not short-term optimization or financial engineering.

Contact: b@mae365.com

Investment Focus

We target service-based businesses tied to infrastructure, mission-critical facilities, public-sector demand, and long-term maintenance cycles—where operational execution, repeat clients, and capable teams drive durable value.

  • Construction and specialty trades
  • Project and construction management
  • Environmental and government-adjacent services

Acquisition Criteria

  • $500K+ in annual cash flow or earnings
  • Established operations with a track record
  • Recurring customers or contracted revenue
  • Capable management or key operators in place
  • Clean financials and straightforward operations

Founder

Brandon Krone is the Founder and Principal of Mae Management LLC. He brings a rare combination of hands-on construction experience and institutional finance expertise.

Brandon grew up working in a family general contracting business, studied architectural design, and competed as a Division I wrestler at the University of Minnesota before building a career in finance at Fidelity Investments and Charles Schwab.

Mae Management was formed as a convergence of these experiences—with a commitment to disciplined ownership, respect for operators, and responsible long-term stewardship.
